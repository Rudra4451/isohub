How to Download macOS

🍎 Download via Mac App Store (Easiest)
Open the App Store on your Mac, search for "macOS Sequoia" and click Get. This is the recommended method.
↗ Apple Support Page
⚙ System Settings Method
Go to Apple Menu → System Settings → General → Software Update to update macOS directly.
↗ Apple Support Page

How to Upgrade macOS

  • 1
    Check compatibility — verify your Mac model is supported (see the list above).
  • 2
    Back up with Time Machine — connect an external drive and back up using Apple's built-in Time Machine app.
  • 3
    Open the Mac App Store, search for "macOS Sequoia" and click Get to start the download.
  • 4
    Once downloaded, the installer launches automatically. Click Continue and follow the on-screen instructions.
  • 5
    Your Mac will restart a few times during installation. This is normal — the process takes 30–60 minutes.
  • 6
    Sign in with your Apple ID and you're all set! Your apps and files will be exactly where you left them.
💡 Tip: macOS upgrades preserve all your files and apps — unlike Windows or Linux installs you don't need to erase anything. Your data stays safe during the upgrade.
