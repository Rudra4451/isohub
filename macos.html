How to Download macOS

Mac App Store (Easiest)
Open App Store on your Mac, search "macOS Sequoia" and click Get.
Apple Support
System Settings Method
Go to Apple Menu → System Settings → General → Software Update.

How to Upgrade macOS

  • 1
    Check compatibility — verify your Mac model is in the list above.
  • 2
    Back up with Time Machine — connect an external drive and run Time Machine backup.
  • 3
    Open the Mac App Store, search "macOS Sequoia" and click Get.
  • 4
    The installer launches automatically. Click Continue and follow the instructions.
  • 5
    Your Mac restarts several times — completely normal. Takes 30–60 minutes total.
  • 6
    Sign in with your Apple ID — all apps and files will be right where you left them!
Tip: macOS upgrades preserve all your files and apps — you don't need to erase anything, unlike Windows/Linux installs.
