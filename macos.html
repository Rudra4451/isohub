How to Upgrade macOS

  • 1
    Check compatibility — verify your Mac model is supported using the list above.
  • 2
    Back up with Time Machine — connect an external drive and back up using Apple's built-in Time Machine.
  • 3
    Open the Mac App Store, search for "macOS Sequoia" and click Get to start the download.
  • 4
    Once downloaded, the installer launches automatically. Click Continue and follow the instructions.
  • 5
    Your Mac will restart a few times during installation. This takes 30–60 minutes.
  • 6
    Sign in with your Apple ID — your apps and files will be exactly where you left them!
Tip: macOS upgrades preserve all your files and apps — unlike Windows/Linux installs, you don't need to erase anything. Your data stays safe!
