Step-by-Step

How to Change Your OS

Follow these 6 steps to safely install any operating system on your laptop or desktop.

Step 01
Download the ISO
Click any button above. You'll go to the official site. Download the ISO file (1–5 GB) and save it somewhere easy to find.
Step 02
Create Bootable USB
Use Rufus (Windows) or balenaEtcher (Mac/Linux) to flash the ISO to a USB drive (8 GB+).
Step 03
Backup Your Files
Copy important files to external storage or cloud. A clean install erases the target drive — don't skip this!
Step 04
Enter Boot Menu
Restart and press F2, F12, DEL, or ESC to open BIOS. Select your USB drive to boot from it.
Step 05
Run the Installer
Follow the on-screen wizard. Choose "Erase disk and install" for a clean fresh start on your machine.
Step 06
Boot Your New OS
Takes 10–30 minutes. Remove USB when prompted, restart — welcome to your brand new operating system!
